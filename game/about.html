About

Hustle is a unique and exciting play-to-earn decentralized game where players can earn tokens and NFTs as rewards for playing. The game is an infinite runner, where the player collects NFTs while navigating through obstacles. The more NFTs the player collects, the greater the reward.

Hustle is designed to be beginner-friendly, with a focus on making Web3 more accessible to all users. Players do not need any technical knowledge to play and enjoy the game, as it provides a fun and engaging way to earn rewards. So whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the world of Web3, Hustle is the perfect way to join the exciting and rapidly growing decentralized gaming community.
